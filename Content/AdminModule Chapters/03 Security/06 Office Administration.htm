Office Administration.

 

Edit Mode: Form and Grid

Note: While you may edit data directly in the grid, it's easier to edit data via the Office Information form.

Description: The Office Administration option allows Administrators to add, modify, or delete offices within the organization, as well as outside agencies and businesses. This aids with system security, but also provides a location to enter useful information (such as the office address). Information entered into this section is displayed in the office information of headers that pull into client forms and letters and is displayed in PDQs, Reports, and Accounts used throughout the system.

Note: Each system may display slightly different fields, depending on the application configuration and system type. Some of the fields in the Office Information form can be made visible or be removed from this form by the Administrator through settings in the Application Settings option.

Refer to the Office Information Form topic for more detailed information on working with the Office Information form.

 

Adding a New Office
  1. Select the Action Row where it says, "Please click here to add a new record..." This opens a new Office Information form.
  2. Fill out the form as you would any other form.

 

 Editing Existing Office Data
  1. Select the Office in the grid to open it's Office Information form.
  2. Edit fields on the form as you would with any other form.
