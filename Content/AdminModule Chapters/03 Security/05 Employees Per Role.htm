Employees Per Role.

 

Edit Mode: No editing

Description: The Employees Per Role option allows Administrators to view employees assigned to each role. This is an informational grid, roles are not changed or added in this grid. The grid is grouped by role and shows the employee's name, title, office number, office name, and grouped by Role name. Employees with multiple roles appear in the grid multiple times, under each role they have.

You can filter, sort, and group (or ungroup) data within the grid.

 

  • Filter the Office column to see the roles for all employees in that office.
  • Ungroup the Role column, then filter by an employee's name to see all roles that employee has.
  • Filter the Role or Role ID column to see all the employees that have that role.
