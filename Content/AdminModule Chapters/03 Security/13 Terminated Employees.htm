Terminated Employees.

 

Edit Mode: Form and Grid

Note: While you may edit data directly in the grid, it's easier to edit data via the Employee Information form.

Description: The Terminated Employees option allows Administrators to track employees within an organization as well as other system users. This aids with both human resource records and establishing system security. In the event of a rehire, Administrators can clear the employee's termination date, change roles for employees with new job responsibilities, change the office assigned to the employee, and assign an employee to a caseload or team that has already been created.

The Terminated Employees options displays a list of all currently terminated employees. A terminated employee is defined as an employee with a termination date in the past. Employees with termination dates of today or in the future will still appear in the Employee Administration table. Selecting an employee record in the table opens the employee's Employee Information form.

 

Editing Existing Employee Data
  1. Select the Employee in the grid to open his/her Employee Information form.
  2. Edit fields on the form as you would with any other form.
