Bid Posted Waiting Time ECID 99016020

This edit check makes sure that the client has been in (20) status for at least the minimum amount of time established by your Agency. The default wait time is 14 days, however this may have been changed according to your Agency's preference. If the minimum wait time has not been met, the following message appears:

"Bid Posted must remain in status for [XX] days. Case can be progressed at [ first date beyond the minimum amount of days]."
