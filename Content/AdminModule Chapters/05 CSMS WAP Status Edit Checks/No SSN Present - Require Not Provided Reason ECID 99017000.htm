No SSN Present - Require Not Provided Reason ECID 99017000

This edit check is associated with the Low Income HEAP, Enhanced Low Income HEAP, and Weatherization Assistance programs. It runs when the case is moved into Pending status. It checks to see if all household members have SSNs on file.

Messages associated with this edit check:

  • The following Household Members have no SSN and require a reason:
  • The following Household Members have refused to provide an SSN, therefore a case may not be opened at this time:
