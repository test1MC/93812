Enable the Forms to Include section of the Account Information form

(App.Account.FormsToLaunch.Enabled)

 

This setting hides or shows the field Forms to be included when this Account is selected. This field is only visible on statewide accounts. It appears below the Account Number field on the form. This setting causes a button to appear on the Authorization form that allows the user to open the selected form. The button title matches the title of the selected form, for example: Open [Form Name].
