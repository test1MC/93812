Term for 'Actual Amount Expended' field on Account form

(App.Account.Field.ActualAmountExpended.Terminology)

 

Allows you to change the terminology for the Actual Amount Expended field on the Account Information form. This is the field that displays amounts from authorization payments that have been approved and batched (sent to be printed on checks) or processed. The default value is Actual Amount Expended. To change it, enter the terminology your Agency uses into the Value field.
