Whether to show the Physical Location Information on the Scanned Document form

(App.ScannedDocument.PhysicalLocationInfo.Visible)

 

Controls the appearance of the Evidence Additional Information section on the form.

As of 03/26/2015 this section includes the following fields:

  • Date Collected
  • Collector Initials
  • Discoverable?
  • Room Location
  • Cabinet Number
  • Drawer Number
