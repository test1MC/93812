Title for the Flagged Checkbox on Scanned Documents

(App.ScannedDocument.FlaggedField.Title)

 

Controls the title/field label of the Flag this document? field on the form. The default Value is Flag this Document? To change it, enter the title your Agency would like to use instead into the Value field. The application setting Show Flagged checkboxfield on Scanned Documents controls the appearance of this field on the form.
