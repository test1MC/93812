Show the Electronic Signature section on the Employee Form

(App.Employee.Field.SignatureAttach.Show)

 

The Electronic Signature section stores a digital signature for the employee. When the system is configured to use stored digital signatures for employees on forms within the system they are stored within this section on the Employee Information form.

This setting controls the appearance of the Electronic Signature section on the Employee Information form. When set to True the section appears on the form above the System Notifications section. When set to False the section doesn't appear on the form.


Electronic Signature section on the Employee Information form.

 

If the option "Use electronic signature pad for and/or guardian signatures and pull in the stored signature for employees" is selected in the When Dating a Signature Use Signature Pad to Capture Electronic Signature application setting and a signature has not been stored in the Employee Information form or if this section is not visible, a message appears to the user prompting them to update their stored signature.

Note: When the user signs electronically as prompted in the above paragraph, the signature is not stored in their Employee Information form; it's only stored on the form being signed. He/she will continue to get that prompt until they have attached a digital signature to their Employee Information form.
