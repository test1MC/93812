Require the bid witnesses on the bid results form

(App.RFQ.BidWitnessesRequired.Enabled)

 

When the Value field is set to True, the Bid Witnesses section of the (1406) form must be completed before it can be approved. If the Bid Witnesses section is not filled in when you sign the form, the following message appears:

Two Bid Witnesses must be present for all Bid Openings.
