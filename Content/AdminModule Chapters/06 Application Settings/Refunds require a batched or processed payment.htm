Refunds require a batched/processed payment

(App.PaymentApproval.Refund.RequireExpenditures)

 

Controls whether or not refunds require a batched/processed payment. When set to true, refunds cannot be entered unless there are payments in an expended status (e.g. batched or processed) at least equal to the amount of the refund. When set to false, the refunds can be tied to expenditures or payments in process.
