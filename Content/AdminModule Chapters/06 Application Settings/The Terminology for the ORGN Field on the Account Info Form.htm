The Terminology for the ORGN Field on the Account information Form

(App.Account.Field.ORGN.Terminology)

 

Allows you to change the terminology for the ORGN field on the form. This the field that displays an account code specific to your Agency. The default value is ORGN. To change it, enter the terminology your Agency uses into the Value field.
