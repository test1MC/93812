When Dating a Signature Use Signature Pad to Capture Electronic Signature

(App.Signature.Use.SignaturePad)

 

Allows you to pick and choose which forms use electronic signature functionality, or globally apply electronic signature rules.

Available Options

  • Do not use electronic signature pad
  • Use electronic signature pad for all signatures
  • Use electronic signature pad for only and/or guardian signatures
  • Use electronic signature pad for and/or guardian signatures and pull in the stored signature for employees
    • If this option is selected, the form will pull the employee signature stored on their Employee Information form. Whether or not this section shows on the Employee Information form is controlled by the application setting Show the Electronic Signature section on the Employee Form.
    • If this section is not visible on the Employee Information form, and no signature has been stored, the employee will receive a prompt each time they attempt to sign a form electronically that they need to update their electronic signature. The system will still permit the employee to sign the form electronically and the form will display the signature captured at the time they sign that particular form.

To Apply Globally

Do not use the Extended Settings, simply select one of the options from the first lookup field.

To Apply To One Form at a Time

Use the Extended Settings to apply electronic signature rules to individual forms. You can set different rules to different forms as well as tie rules to specific groups of users by adding User Roles to the ACL field. This is a multiple-section section, you may add as many forms as you would like. Refer to Application Setting Form for more information on filling out the Extended Settings section of the Application Setting form.

Note: Not all forms that require signatures will automatically work with electronic signatures. They must have the correct signature block. If you configure a form to use electronic signatures and it doesn't work as expected, please contact. Libera.
