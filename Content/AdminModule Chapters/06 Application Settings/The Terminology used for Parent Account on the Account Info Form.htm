The Terminology used for Parent Account on the Account Information Form

(App.Account.Parent.Terminology)

 

Allows you to change the terminology for the Parent Account field on the Account Information form. This field displays the account name of the parent account. It appears on the form for the sub-account. The default value is Parent Account. To change it, enter the terminology your Agency uses into the Value field
