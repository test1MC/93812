Lock the Account field on the Payment Approval

(App.PaymentApproval.Field.Account.ReadOnly)

 

When set to True, the Account field on the form is locked against editing so the user is forced to use the default account. Your agency can configure exceptions to this setting in the Extended Settings section. For example it's not uncommon to allow designated users such as System Administrators or fiscal employees the ability to edit the Account field.
