Term for 'Payments in Process' field on Account form

(App.Account.Field.PaymentsInProcess.Terminology)

 

Allows you to change the terminology for the Payments in Process field on the Account Information form. This is the field that displays the total of payment approvals that haven't been marked as expended (payments less amount vouched). The default value is Payments in Process. To change it, enter the terminology your Agency uses into the Value field.
