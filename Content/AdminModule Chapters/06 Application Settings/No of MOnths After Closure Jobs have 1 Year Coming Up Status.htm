No. of Months after Closure Jobs have 1 Year Coming Up Status in Service Agreement Jobs PDQ

(App.KEEM.1YearServiceAgreement.Review.ShowUp.Months)

 

The number in the Value field represents the number of months since closure date. This number is used to calculate the Job's service agreement status as of the date the PDQ is run.

This setting controls when the appears in the 1 Year Coming Up status.
