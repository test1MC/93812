Show the KEEM Job Number on the bid results form

(App.RFQ.ShowKeemJobNumber.Enabled)

 

Controls whether the Job Number or a unique ID field shows on the (1406) form. When the Value field is set to True, the Number is displayed. Otherwise the unique ID number for the application is displayed.
