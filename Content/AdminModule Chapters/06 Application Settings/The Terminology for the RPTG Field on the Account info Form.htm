The Terminology for the RPTG Field on the Account information Form

(App.Account.Field.RPTG.Terminology)

 

Allows you to change the terminology for the RPTG field on the Account Information form. This field displays an account code specific to your Agency. The default value is RPTG. To change it, enter the terminology your Agency uses into the Value field.
