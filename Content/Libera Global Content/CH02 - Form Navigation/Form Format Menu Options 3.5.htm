Form Format Menu Options

The Format menu is only available when cursor focus is in a memo field or an editable region.

 


Format menu.

 

  • Bold - Bolds selected text. Alternatively you may press CTRL+B.
  • Italic - Italicizes selected text. Alternatively you may press ALT+I.
  • Underline - Underlines selected text. Alternatively you may press ALT+U.
  • Superscript - Superscripts selected text.
  • Subscript - Subscripts selected text.
  • Justify Left - Lines up selected text on the left margin
  • Justify Center - Centers selected text.
  • Justify Right - Lines up text on the right margin.
  • Justify Full - Spreads out selected text evenly from right to left.
  • Ordered List - Sequentially numbers and indents selected text.
  • Unordered List - Bullets and indents selected text.
  • Indent - Moves selected text to the right.
  • Outdent - Moves selected text to the left.
