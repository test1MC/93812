Multiple-Section Sections in Forms


A multiple-section section highlighted yellow.

Many forms have sections that allow you to add multiple sections. A multiple-section section is indicated when the background color of the section changes to pale yellow after initial data entry. Screen readers alert users when they enter a multiple-section section of a form.

Regardless of the data in the section or the size of the section, the process of adding and deleting multiple-section sections is the same. For example, where X denotes the section you want to add to or delete from:

Adding a Multiple-Section Section

Place cursor in any field in the X section. Then open the Tools menu and select the Add X… option. Or, with cursor focus in the X section, press the hotkey CTRL+ALT+Plus Sign.


Example of a multiple-section section with two sections.

Deleting a Multiple-Section Section

Place cursor in any field in the X section. Then open the Tools menu and select the Delete X… option. Or, with cursor focus in the section you with to delete, press the hotkey CTRL+ALT+Minus Sign.
