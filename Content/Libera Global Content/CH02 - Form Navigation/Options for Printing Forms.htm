Options for Printing Forms



The Form Print Options dialog window.

 

Use the Print Options option on the File menu to customize how the form looks when printed, sent, or saved locally.

Note: Making changes in your browser page set up dialog window affects all pages accessed via your browser, including those outside of System 7™.

 

General Settings
  • Zoom Percentage - Set the zoom percentage to meet the needs of the recipient of the printed form.

 

 Field Box Options
  • No Boxes - Form prints without any boxes around fields, dropdown buttons, etc. This is the default setting.
  • Underline Only - Form prints with data entry fields underlined. When printing the form to be filled out by hand, this option is a visual aid to field location and provides plenty of space for large handwriting.
  • Data Entry Boxes Only - Prints form displaying boxes around all data entry fields. Provides a visual aid when filling out the form by hand.
  • All Boxes - The form prints with all field boxes, data entry boxes, and drop down buttons as viewed on the screen.

 

 Memo Field Options
  • Minimum Memo Field Lines - Sets the minimum number of blank lines to print for memo fields. Adjust to provide more space for handwritten responses or provide less lines to save paper when printing.

 

 Buttons
  • Page Setup - Press SPACEBAR or click to open your browser's Page Setup dialog window. Alternatively you may press ALT+G.
  • Preview - Press SPACEBAR or click to open your browser's Print Preview window. Alternatively you may press ALT+V. Use this option to see how your selections change the look of the printed form.
  • Print - Press SPACEBAR or click to open your operating system's Print dialog window. Alternatively you may press ALT+P.
  • OK - Press SPACEBAR or click to apply your changes. Alternatively you may press ALT+K.
  • Cancel - Press SPACEBAR to cancel your changes. Alternatively you may press ALT+C.

 
  • You may need to adjust your browser's Page Setup settings manually when increasing the zoom percentage.
  • When the zoom percentage is very large (in excess of ~175% depending on font size of original) the text does not wrap. Instead the form prints on multiple sheets of paper that can be taped together to make the form readable.
  • You may consider printing on larger paper when using larger zoom percentages.
