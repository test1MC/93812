Form Edit Menu Options


Edit menu.

 

  • Undo - As long as you are still in the field, this un-does the changes you just made. Alternatively you may press CTRL+Z. If you have moved to another field you may not undo the change you made.
  • Redo - As long as you are still in the field and if you have un-done changes, this re-does the changes you made. If you have moved to another field you may not redo the change.
  • Cut - Erases selected text. Places text on your clipboard for pasting. Copied text is available until you copy or cut other text. Alternatively you may press CTRL+X.
  • Copy - Copies selected text. Places text on your clipboard for pasting. Copied text is available until you copy or cut other text. Alternatively you may press CTRL+C.
  • Paste - Pastes selected text from your clipboard to a selected field in a form. Alternatively, you may press CTRL+V.
  • Paste from Word - Pastes text from Word converting Word formatting code to work with HTML.
  • Paste Text - Pastes text into selected field on form while stripping all formatting from original source, for example a word processing or email program.
  • Select All - Selects everything on the form for editing, copying, or cutting. Amount selected varies depending on cursor location at the time this option is selected. Alternatively you may press CTRL+A.
  • Find - Opens Find Text dialog window. Use to find specific text throughout the form.

Find Text Dialog Window


Find Text dialog window.

  • Find What - type word or part of word that you are looking for.
  • Find Next - Activates the find functionality.
  • Match Case - When selected, it will only look for text with the same case entered in the Find What field.
  • Match whole word only - When selected, it will not find partial matches.
  • Close - Closes the Find Text dialog window.
  • Go To Field - Opens grid displaying all fields on the form. By default cursor focus is on the field that had focus at the time you selected Go To Field. Use the mouse or Arrow keys to navigate within the grid. Double-clicking or pressing ENTER on a field closes the grid and moves the cursor to that field within the form.

    Go To Field list window.
  • Extended Editor - Opens a word processor memo displaying the contents of your memo field. Allows for more formatting options. Alternatively when the cursor is in the memo field, press CTRL+ENTER. Once the Extended Editor is open save your changes to close it and return to your form.

    Extended Editor window.
