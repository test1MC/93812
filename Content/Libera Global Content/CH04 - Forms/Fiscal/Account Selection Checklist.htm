Account Selection Checklist

Are you setting up an account for the first time, filling out an authorization, or receiving a notification regarding an account? Here is a checklist that you can use to ensure that the form is filled out correctly and can be used in the system as desired.

Note: You might not have the appropriate user role to do some of the steps listed below. If that is the case, please check with your Supervisor.

  • To ensure that the account is on, the Turn Account Off? field must be "N"
  • For Office/ - one of the following must be true:
    • The is 0 (statewide) and the Office is 00 (central office)
    • The matches the of the 's office and the office is 00 (central office)
    • The Office matches the 's office
  • For Program - one of the following must be true:
    • The Program of the account matches the program of the Authorization
    • The Program of the account is Supported Employment, the Authorization is a VR Authorization, and the case is a Supported Employment case
    • The Program of the account is 0 (General) and the service requires a specific Account Identifier or Account Number
  • For Special Project - one of the following must be true (if used by your Agency):
    • The account has the same Special Project as the
    • The account has a Special Project matching the Default Project Description for the program
    • The account does not have a Special Project, the does not have one and there is no Default Project Description for the program of the Authorization
    • If multiple accounts have a suitable Special Project, then your agency may use Rank to determine which to use. See the Form Help for the Account Information Form for more information.
  • Account Identifier (from the form - Allowable Statuses section. Check the form for the Agency Authorization Account Identifier) - one of the following must be true:
    • The Account Identifier of the account matches that of the service
    • The Account Identifier does not match the Account Identifier specified for the service; the Take money only from this account? field is not checked and no accounts for the specified Account Identifier are available
    • The service does not have an Account Identifier specified
  • For Account Number - one of the following must be true for Contracts (this overrides Office, Region, Program, Special Project, and Account Identifier):
    • The Account Number of the account matches that of the contract except the fiscal year (which are replaced with asterisks in the contract)
    • The Contract does not have an Account Number specified
    • The Service is not contracted
  • If the Only available if the service specifies this Account Identifier or Account Number? checkbox on the Account is Y, then the Account is not valid except for services that specify an Account Identifier or Account Number as above.
  • For Authorizations - all of the following must be true:
    • The Service Begin Date must be within the Begin Date and End Date of the account
    • The of the account must be greater than or equal to the amount being authorized
    • Only the highest ranked account(s) will be available (unless the Account Number on Authorizations is Read-Only application setting is set to False).
  • For forms - all of the following must be true:
    • Date Rendered on the form must be within the Begin Date and End Date of the account
    • Date Expired (Payments) on the form must be prior to today's date.
    • If the account differs from the authorization, then the new account must have a greater than or equal to the amount of the payment.
