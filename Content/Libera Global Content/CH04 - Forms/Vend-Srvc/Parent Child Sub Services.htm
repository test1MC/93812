The use of Parent Service and Child/Sub Services allows Agencies to categorize services. It also allows users to plan services at the highest level and then authorize at the lowest level. This is handy when you don’t know exactly the specific services a will need when you are developing their Plan.

Level 1 = Parent Service, think Category.

  • For example, Dental Consultation/Evaluation/Procedures Parent Service

Level 2 = Child/Sub Service, think Service.

  • ABUT Support Crown Titanium (D6094)
  • Abutment Supported Crown (D6058)
  • Amalgam One Surface Permanent (D2140)
  • Bridge Resin Base Metal (D6251)

Following our example above, if an needs dental work, but at plan development you don’t know which specific service he/she may need, you can plan to the parent service, Dental Consultation/Evaluation/Procedures Parent Service. Then later, when you are authorizing services you will be able to select from all the services linked to the parent service without resulting in a Plan Amendment.
