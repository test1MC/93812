Example of a completed Invoice Review form.

Overview

The form is added when you select the Invoice Review... option from the Tools menu of an authorization form. When you fill it out and close it, it's stored in the ECF.

The form is added when you select a payment option from the Tools menu of an authorization form. When you fill it out and close it, it's stored in the ECF.

Form Fields

The following fields are required before you save the payment approval:

  • Vendor Invoice Number
  • Date Services Completed
  • Date Approved for Payment
