Transfer Authorizations Tool


tool.

 

Overview

Use the tool to transfer authorization transactions between accounts.

 

 

Getting to the

  1. Log on with rights to access the .
  2. Open the File Drawer to the Tools tab.
  3. Filter the Tools tab by the partial word trans (short for transfer), then select the option to open the tool.

 

Form Functionality

  • Use the Accounts to Transfer from field to select one or more accounts from which to transfer authorizations and/or payments. Refer to Multiple-Section Sections in Forms for more information. on working with multiple-sections.
  • The transfer tool displays both authorization records and payment records.

    • When selecting an authorization record to transfer, it moves all the encumbrance (but not liquidation) for that line item associated with the selected account, except those tied to payments.

    • When selecting a payment record to transfer, it moves the encumbrance, payment and amount warranted for that specific payment to the new account.

  • The Authorizations to Transfer lookup shows the following based on the Show Warranted? and Show Unwarranted? fields:
    • Show Warranted? Show Unwarranted? Results
      Y Y All Payments and Authorizations with outstanding balances
      Y N Only warranted payments
      N Y Unwarranted Payments and Authorizations with outstanding balances
      N N Nothing

  • If a deactivated account is selected from the Account to Transfer To lookup, an informational message appears letting you know the selected "to" account is deactivated. You may still use this account or change the "to" account.

  • Use the Amount to Transfer field to limit the authorizations returned in the Authorizations to transfer lookup to authorizations that add up to (as close as possible) the amount entered. The authorizations returned are based on the service Begin Date. The payments returned are based on the . In both cases, it starts with the oldest dates. If you leave the field blank, it will show all authorizations and payments tied to the selected "from" accounts.
  • The Document Number field is used for internal tracking and is completely optional. This value appears in the PDQ after the transfer is completed.
  • The Comments field is used for any comments you would like to add and is completely optional. This value appears in the PDQ after the transfer is completed.

  • When transferring to an upper level account, the sub-account to use is automatically determined. However, if the target account does not have a corresponding sub-account, the entire transfer will fail. Corresponding accounts are based on and Office on the form.

  • Corresponding accounts are also based on the County field on the form. This is controlled by the Enable counties in Account structure application setting.

  • The Total Amounts transferring to section automatically fills in based on the Authorizations to transfer field. The fields display calculated totals based on the authorizations and payments selected from the lookup.

  • Use the Transfer authorizations button to complete the transfer of authorizations between accounts.

  • The Open Account List button opens a new window which displays the Account List PDQ.

  • Use the Cancel Transfer button to cancel the transfer and close the tool.
