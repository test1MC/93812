Change Your Password



Change Your Password window.

 

Overview

The Change Your Password tool is an option on the Tools menu of the File Drawer.

 

Form Fields

  • Your Name – Automatically populated with your name. You may not edit this field.
  • Current Password – This field is selected by default when the form opens. Type your current password here. As you type your password only dots or asterisks will be displayed.
  • New Password – Type your new password. Passwords must meet the complexity level requirement set by your Agency. As you type your new password only dots or asterisks will be displayed.
  • Confirm New Password – Confirm your new password by typing it again.
  • Save Password – Select this button to save your new password. When your new password is successfully changed the following message will appear: Your password was successfully changed. There are several reasons that your new password may not be accepted including your new password is too similar to your old password, your new password confirmation does not match, or your new password doesn’t meet password requirements and more. If this happens a message will appear informing you what was wrong. Select OK on the message window to be returned to the Change Your Password window and try again.
  • Cancel – Select this button to cancel your password change.

When you have successfully changed your password select the OK button. This closes the message and the Change Your Password window.
