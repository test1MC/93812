Copy Budget Accounts to New Year


Copy Budge Accounts to New Year tool.

 

Overview

Use the Copy Budget Account to New Year tool to quickly create budget accounts for the following fiscal year. The tool copies everything except the amount and sets the Turn Account Off field to Y (Yes). You need to provide the amount and set the field Turn Account Off? to N (No) before the new account can be used in authorizations.

 

Steps

  1. Log on with rights to access the Copy Budget Accounts to New Year tool.
  2. Open the File Drawer to the Tools tab.
  3. Select the Copy Budget Accounts to New Year option from the list. This action opens the tool.
  4. Year to copy accounts from – Type the fiscal year from which you are copying accounts. If the name of any account being copied contains the fiscal year in the Account Name, the year will be changed to match that of the new fiscal year. The rest of the Account Name will not be changed. This fiscal year must be before the fiscal year of the Year to copy accounts to field.
  5. Year to copy accounts to – Type the fiscal year for which you are creating accounts. The number of years between the Year to copy accounts from and the Year to copy accounts to is added to the following dates on the form:
    • Begin Date
    • End Date
    • Date Expired (Payments)
  6. Note: If at least one account from the year to copy from already exists in the year to copy to, none of the accounts will be copied. A message will notify you which accounts already exist in the new fiscal year.
  7. When both years have been entered, the Copy Accounts to New Year button becomes active. Select this button to create the accounts. You’ll receive a confirmation message regarding how many accounts were copied.
