Overview

The PDQ returns a list of all employees that meet the following criteria:

  • They do not have a termination date entered on their Employee Information form.
  • They are associated with an Office.
  • That Office is in turn associated with a .

The grid will then show the last login date and time for each employee if this data is showing on their Employee Information form. The data is grouped by first by and then by Office.
