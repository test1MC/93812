Example of the form.

Overview

The form is used to determine if weatherization measures are needed or not. It first becomes available when the is in status. Completing the form results in the generation of the letter.

Form Functionality

  • Assigned on - Completing this field results in the letter automatically opening. The automatically launches if a is selected as the Auditor Name.
  • Blower door test performed? - When the response is Y (Yes), you must provide the Reading. When the response is N (No) you must Specify the reason the test was not performed.
  • Client Education Provided? - When the response is Y (Yes), you must Specify one or more options. Refer to Multiple Select Lookups and Grids for more information.
  • Pre-Audit determination - This field determines the next status for a upon signing the form.
  • Category (Attachment) - If you select the Pre-Audit Pictures of the Home option, the Pictures Provided? field automatically displays a Y (Yes); otherwise it displays N (No).
  • Approved? and Signature Date - Filling in these fields "signs" the form. When you complete the Signature Date and move out of the field these fields and the form above the signature line locks against further editing. The Signature Date is also the status date of the next status.
  • Pre-Audit Kilowatt-hour (kWh) Information - The information collected in the fields in this section is used to compare against the post-audit results to show whether the weatherization measures were successful or not.
