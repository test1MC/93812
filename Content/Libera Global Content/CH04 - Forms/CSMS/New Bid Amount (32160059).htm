Overview

Use the form to change the amount of the winning bid. Get to the form via the Change Winning Bid Amount button on the form. The button appears after the form has been approved.

Form Functionality

  • Current Bid Amount - Displays the current Bid Amount of the winning on the form.
  • Current Bid Reason - When it's the first change, it displays "Initial Bid." After it's been changed it displays the New Bid Reason from the previous change.
  • New Bid Amount - Enter the new bid amount. The new amount shows in the Bid Amount field of the winning on the form. It also shows in the Bid History PDQ which opens when you select the View Winning Bid History button on the form.
  • New Bid Reason - Enter the reason for the new bid. Your entry shows in the Bid History PDQ which opens when you select the View Winning Bid History button on the form.
