Overview

The form is used to determine if weatherization measures were completed satisfactorily or not. It becomes available when the is in status. Completing the form results in the generation of the letter.

Form Functionality

  • reported completed - In the first instance of the form, this auto fills with the date the moved into status. In the second instance of the form, this auto fills with the date from the Date corrections submitted field of the first instance of the form.
  • Assigned on - Completing this field results in the letter automatically opening. The automatically launches if a is selected as the Auditor Name.
  • Post-Audit determination - This field determines the next status for a upon signing the form.
  • Failure Reason - Field appears when the Post-Audit determination is "Fail." You must provide one or more reasons for failure. Refer to Multiple Select Lookups and Grids for more information.
  • Category (Attachment) - If you select the Pre-Audit Pictures of the Home option, the Pictures Provided? field automatically displays a Y (Yes); otherwise it displays N (No).
  • Approved? and Signature Date - Filling in these fields "signs" the form. When you complete the Signature Date and move out of the field these fields and the form above the signature line locks against further editing. The Signature Date is also the status date of the next status.
  • Pre-Audit Kilowatt-hour (kWh) Information - The information collected in the fields in this section comes from the form. The information is used to compare against the Post-Audit Kilowatt-hour (kWh) Information to show whether the weatherization measures were successful or not.
  • Post-Audit Kilowatt-hour (kWh) Information - Enter the Post-Weatherization 12 month kWh Usage amount 12 months after weatherization measures were completed. Actual Energy Savings calculates based on this entry. Actual expenditures and Actual Cost per Square Foot are automatically populated when s are processed by designated staff via the PDQ.
