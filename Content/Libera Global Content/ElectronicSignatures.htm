Electronic Signatures

If your Agency has opted to use signature pads to obtain signatures electronically, you or your , will be prompted to physically sign the signature pad. All other signature functionality on the form remains unchanged.

Electronic signature images are visible in form snapshots used by Print Layout, Reading Layout, form/element snapshots (used by letters, etc. when signing to lock the form contents), Save Locally, etc.

When you clear a signature date on the form, the electronic signature will also be cleared.

Note: The signature image will not be available to view (may show a red X placeholder) if you run the "Save Locally" or "Send To" options, or load the "Print Layout" and save the HTML file for later use. It is also not available in Text Layout view, etc.

 

Supported Signature Pad Models

System 7™supports the following Topaz signature pad models:

  • T-LBK750SE-BHSB-R model (SigGemLite 4x3 SE (preferred) and non-SE models)
  • T-L462-HSB-R model (SignatureGemLCD 1x5)
  • T-LBK460-HSB-R model (SigLite LCD 1x5)
