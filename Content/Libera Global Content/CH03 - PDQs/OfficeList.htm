PDQ with the Data Tree expanded.

 

Overview

The PDQ opens a list of all offices to which you have access. The PDQ displays the office name and contact information. Once you have selected an office you will also be able to access the Office Information Form and any other forms related to the selected office via the Electronic Case Folder (ECF).

 

PDQ Results

  • All information displayed in the PDQ results comes from the Office Information Form.
  • LOID is optional data for location ID.
  • The Office Calendar button loads the calendar for the office selected in the Data Tree in a new window.
