Active and Approved Service/Vendor List


Active and Approved Service/Vendor List PDQ.

 

Overview

The Active and Approved Service/Vendor List PDQ displays a list of vendors grouped by service. Expanding a service loads the vendors that provide that service in a child table.

 

PDQ Parameters

  • Deactivated/Unapproved? - This field determines whether the deactivated and unapproved services will be included in the results. When N (No) is entered, only the services that have both an active and approved form and an active and approved form on file will be displayed in the Data Tree. When Y (Yes) is entered, all services, approved and unapproved, are displayed in the Data Tree.
  • You must make a selection.

 

PDQ Results

  • Data Tree results are grouped by service. Expand the groups to see the associated vendors listed below. To expand or collapse a group, either single-click the plus or minus sign to the left of the row or DOWN ARROW to the row and press the SPACEBAR.
  • Selecting a vendor in the Data Tree loads the vendor's ECF.
  • Selecting a service in the Data Tree loads the service's ECF.
