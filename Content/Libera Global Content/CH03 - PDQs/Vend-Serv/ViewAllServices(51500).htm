View All Services


View All Services PDQ.

 

Overview

The View All Services PDQ displays all services within system. All being defined as services for all programs, active and deactivated, linked to a vendor or not.

 

PDQ Results

  • All of the data displayed in the Data Tree comes from the form. Note: Not all of the information on the form appears in the grid.
  • Selecting a service record in the Data Tree opens the form and loads the ECF for the service.
  • Add a new service via the Add New Service button available at the bottom of the Data Tree. Single-click with the mouse or press CTRL+B to jump to the buttons, DOWN ARROW until you reach the button, then press ENTER. This opens a blank form.
