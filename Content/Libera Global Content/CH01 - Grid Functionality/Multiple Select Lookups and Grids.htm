Multiple Select Lookups and Grids

Multiple Select Lookups

Many lookups and grids give you the ability to select more than one option at a time.

Lookups



Example of a multiple select lookup.

  • To select options one at a time, single-click the check box of each desired option or with cursor focus in the grid, press CTRL+SPACEBAR, in any cell of each record you want to select. Press ENTER or double-click the text of a selected option to close the lookup window and load your selections into the form.
  • To select all options at one time, single-click check box in the column header row or with cursor focus in the grid, press CTRL+SHIFT+SPACEBAR. Press ENTER or double-click the text of a selected option to close the lookup window and load your selections into the form.
  • You may not add an option more than once.
  • If the lookup has an option for "other," a new may field appear in the form when selected. The new field allows you to explain, define, or describe what "other" is.

Data Tree Grids


Example of a multiple select grid in the Data Tree.

  • To select options one at a time, single-click the check box of each desired option or with cursor focus in the grid, press CTRL+SPACEBAR, in any cell of each record you want to select. After you have selected one or more rows, you can use one of the buttons located at the bottom of the Data Tree.
  • To select all options at one time, single-click the check box in the upper left hand corner or with cursor focus in the grid, press CTRL+SHIFT+SPACEBAR. After you have selected all rows, you can use one of the buttons located at the bottom of the Data Tree.
