Filtering is your friend!

 

Some of the grids you encounter in System 7™ will hold hundreds, if not thousands or hundreds of thousands, of records. You do not want to scroll or arrow through all of those records to find what you are looking for.

The fastest way to search for data is via the Filter Row. You will notice that the first row of almost every grid you encounter in System 7™ is the Filter Row. In addition to location, the small blue filter button in each cell denotes the Filter Row. You may filter on a single column or multiple columns.
