JAWS 12 and 13 Don't Read Suggested Words in Spell Check Dialog

When navigating within the suggested words list of the Spell Check Dialog, JAWS reads, "Suggestions left paren alt plus s right paren list box 1 of 25" instead of the suggested word. As a work around, Freedom Scientific suggests using the INSERT+PAGE UP keys to read the suggested words. Press INSERT+PAGE UP keys twice, quickly to spell the suggested words..
