Login Page Doesn't Allow Data Entry

On the Login page, if you cannot immediately type into the Username or Password fields or you must press ENTER to start the screen reader “forms mode,” the screen reader is most likely not configured to work with System 7™.

Current Solution:

Please walk through the steps outlined for your specific screen reader in either the Preparing JAWS® or the Preparing Window-Eyes® section to double-check that your screen reader is set up correctly.
