Window-Eyes Speaks Unwanted Formatting Information

Window-Eyes says extra speech such as “cell” and “custom control” while in System 7™.

Current Solution (Window-Eyes version 7+):

To remove this extra speech, follow these steps while within System 7™:

  1. Press INSERT+V to open Window-Eyes Verbosity Menu.
  2. Press the ARROW keys to navigate within the Groups list, then select Focused in the Groups list.
  3. Press the TAB key to navigate to the Focused check box.
  4. Press the TAB key to enter the sub-options list.
  5. Use the ARROW keys to navigate to the Don’t Include Window Type item. It is then automatically selected.
  6. Press the TAB key once to navigate into the next group.
  7. Press the DOWN ARROW key to navigate to, “Custom Control”.
  8. Press the SPACEBAR to clear, “Custom Control.”
  9. Press the TAB key to navigate to the button, OK. Press SPACEBAR to activate the button. The Verbosity Settings dialog window closes and the changes are saved.
